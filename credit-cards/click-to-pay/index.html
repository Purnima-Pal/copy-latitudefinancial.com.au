How to checkout with Click to Pay

Step 1

Look for the Click to Pay icon at participating online retailers.

Step 2

Enter in your registered mobile number and/or email address at checkout. This may vary according to the retailer.

Step 3

Enter the one-time verification code from Mastercard.

Step 4

Select your Latitude card to checkout. For security, enter the 3-digit CVC for your first transaction.

How to set up Click to Pay

  1. Open the Latitude App and select your account, click on the small card icon in the top right corner
  2. Choose ‘Add Click to Pay’.
  3. All done! Click to Pay uses your mobile number and email address. Your details will appear in the Click to Pay profile section and will be used during checkout.

Frequently Asked Questions

To manage your Click to Pay details go to the Latitude App, select the Card and tap Manage Cards then Click to Pay. You’ll have the option to:

  • View your Click to Pay email and phone number for the Latitude card that has been selected
  • View if your Latitude card is added to Click to Pay
  • Opt-out of Click to Pay for the Latitude card that has been selected

To update your email address and phone number, log into Latitude App, go to Support icon, under Preferences menu, click Manage Personal Details. Any update to your email or mobile number will take a day to update your Click to Pay email or phone number, but we are working on providing these features real time.

Click to Pay is a secure online checkout payment option. With Click to Pay, you can make a purchase using your Latitude card without giving the online store your card number, expiry, or CVC. Instead, you provide your registered Click to Pay email and phone number.

Customers must:

  • Be the account holder aged 18 years and older

  • Hold a valid Australian residential address

  • Be registered in the Latitude App and/or the Latitude Service Centre

  • Hold an eligible and activated Latitude credit card

You can add the following activated Latitude cards to Click to Pay via the Latitude app.

  • Latitude Low Rate Mastercard

  • Latitude 28° Global Platinum Mastercard

  • Latitude GO Mastercard

  • David Jones credit cards

  • Latitude Mastercard

  • Latitude Eco Mastercard

Some Latitude cards aren't eligible for enrolment into Click to Pay via the Latitude app:

  • Latitude Gem Visa

  • Latitude Infinity Rewards Visa

  • Creditline credit cards

  • Buyer's Edge

  • Care Credit

  • Gem Visa (New Zealand product)

  • Gem Essential (New Zealand product)
