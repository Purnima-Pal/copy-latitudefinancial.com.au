Pay over time with Latitude Interest Free payment plans

There are several ways to pay when you choose a long-term Interest Free payment plan at 100s of retailer partners. Flexible plan lengths might differ by store.

Always equal

Equal Monthly plan

  • Keep it simple and pay the same amount every month.

  • Total plan amount is paid in the agreed number of monthly payments.

  • Gives you flexibility to pay out early without any penalty.

Pay extra anytime

Minimum Monthly plan

  • Available provided you pay your credit card minimum payment each month.

  • Gives you flexibility to add extra when possible.

  • Paying this way may not clear the plan within your plan period.

  • Interest applies on any outstanding balance at the end of your plan period.

Pay anytime

Deferred Payment plan

  • No need to pay anything upfront.

  • Make payments at any time it suits you during plan period.

  • Interest applies on any outstanding balance at the end of your plan period.

Payment Options

Latitude App

Make repayments in just a few taps with our mobile app.

  1. Get the app

    Simply download the Latitude App from the App Store or Google Play

  2. Create a Latitude ID

    Open the app and create your Latitude ID (this is different to your Online Service Centre login).

  3. Link your card

    Once your credit cards are linked, you’ll be able to make payments via bank transfer with your BSB and Account Number (Australia only).

Note Please be aware that bank transfer payments can take three business days to clear and you can only make one payment to your card account every five days.

No Fees

We do not charge a fee for payments made via direct bank transfer on the Latitude App.

Latitude Service Centre

One of the quickest & easiest way to make repayments.

Login Register

To make a payment

  1. Login to the Latitude Service Centre
  2. Click ‘Pay My Account’

    Then enter your bank details.

Registering

Registering is easy and only takes a few minutes, just make sure you have your card or 16-digit account number on hand.

Note Please be aware that payments can take 3 to 5 business days to clear.

No Fees

We do not charge a fee for payments made via the Latitude Service Centre.

BPAY®

Available 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

To make a payment

  1. Quote the Biller code

    Latitude GO Mastercard Biller Code (443887)

  2. Your account number
  3. The amount you wish to pay

Note If you’re paying by BPAY on your due date, you must complete the transaction before your bank’s cut off time, otherwise the transaction may be processed on the following day. Some BPAY payments can take up to three days to appear on your account.

Fees

A payment handling fee of $1.95 applies for all payments made via BPAY® to pay your account.
